Does this replace my comprehensive insurance?

No, our membership complements your comprehensive insurance and provides an affordable way to protect your car from everyday scratches and dents. Comprehensive insurance is still essential for covering major damage, theft, or total loss.

Keep in mind that using your comprehensive insurance for minor repairs can lead to losing your no-claim bonus and increased premiums each year. Our membership helps you avoid these downsides by covering the smaller, more frequent damages whilst protecting the resale value of your car and ensuring your most loved asset remains in showroom condition

How does the service work?

You become an Assured member for just $35/pm and you get unlimited scratch and dent repairs from the comfort of your home or office. You simply contact us and we come to you to fix the damage on your car, as often as you need.

Are there any hidden fees or charges?

Depending on your plan there may be a small $50 repair fee . However , we offer plans that don’t have the repair fee. So, its up to you ! If you do go ahead with the $50 repair fee that is still a fraction of what you will pay with insurance and smash repair centres!

What kind of damage is covered by the service?

Bumper Scrapes, Windscreen chips, interior repairs, wheel repairs, deep scratches, dents, light scratches. Our programs cover the every day bumps and scratches on your vehicle, see vehicle diagram and terms and conditions for size limits

Is existing damage covered?

No, only new damage will be covered under your membership. However as a member you are entitled to up to 50% off any existing repairs. This way you can get your car back to showroom condition affordably and then be protected for the future!
